# Pricing Feature Matrix

## Published Plans

### Free

Best for trying Dotly.

Includes:

- 1 digital card
- Public card page
- QR sharing
- Up to 3 social links
- Basic lead capture
- 7-day analytics
- Dotly link/domain

### Starter

Best for individuals and freelancers.

Includes everything in Free, plus:

- 1 premium card
- 30-day analytics
- richer profile content
- more social links
- basic CRM access
- email signature
- email templates
- basic scheduling

Price:

- `10 USDT / month`
- `50 USDT / 6 months`
- `99 USDT / year`

### Pro

Best for professionals and power users.

Includes everything in Starter, plus:

- up to 3 cards
- advanced analytics
- CSV export
- custom domains
- webhooks
- fuller CRM workflows
- inbox and scheduling workflows

Price:

- `20 USDT / month`
- `99 USDT / 6 months`
- `199 USDT / year`
