## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

**Dotly is the modern digital business card built for professionals who mean business.**

Forget paper cards that get lost or thrown away. With Dotly, your contact information, social links, and professional brand live on a beautifully designed digital card — always up to date, always in your pocket.

### Share Your Card Anywhere

- **NFC Tap** — Hold your phone near any NFC-enabled device and share your card instantly. No app required on the recipient's side.
- **QR Code** — Generate a custom-branded QR code for in-person meetings, presentations, email signatures, or print materials.
- **Link Share** — Every Dotly card gets a unique URL (e.g. dotly.one/yourname). Share it via iMessage, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, or anywhere online.

### Capture & Convert Leads

Every time someone visits your card, they can submit their contact info with a single tap. Dotly automatically:
- Creates a contact in your built-in CRM pipeline
- Notifies you instantly via push notification ("New Lead! Alex Chen just saved your card")
- Sends a follow-up email notification so you never miss a connection

### Built-in CRM Pipeline

Move leads through your pipeline from **New → Contacted → Qualified → Closed**. Add notes, track conversation history, and send emails directly from the app — all without switching to another tool.

### Customise Your Card

Choose from multiple professional templates — Minimal, Bold, Creative, or Corporate. Customise colours, fonts, and your background. Add your social media profiles, portfolio links, video reels, and more. Your card is your brand.

### Team & Enterprise Ready

Manage your whole team's digital cards from one dashboard. Apply brand lock to ensure consistent colours and logos across your organisation. Invite members, track analytics per card, and export lead data.

### Analytics That Matter

See exactly who viewed your card, where they came from, and which links they clicked. Track lead submission rates over time. Understand what's working and double down.

### Privacy First

Dotly never sells your data. Your contacts and leads belong to you. Export them any time in standard formats. Full GDPR compliance.
